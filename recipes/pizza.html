  • Pizza Dough 600gr
  • A can of tomatoes
  • Mozzarella for pizza
  • Basil leaves
  • Salt, pepper and olive oil
  1. Oil a round pan and spread the pizza dough
  2. Break the tomatoes with the hands and season the sauce made
  3. Bake at 250C for 15 minutes
  4. Take off Pizza and add the mozzarella cheese
  5. Few more minutes in the oven and then garnish it. Buon appetito.
